00 — Who We Are

Born in the dark net, forged in competition

HACKALYPSE is a Capture The Flag team operating at the bleeding edge of cybersecurity. We don't just solve challenges — we dismantle them, reverse-engineer their architecture, and weaponize our knowledge.

Founded in 2025, we've competed in over 13+ international CTF competitions, ranging from DEFCON and PicoCTF to regional qualifiers and private invitational events.

Our team spans disciplines — from binary exploitation and reverse engineering to cryptography, web exploitation, and OSINT. No flag is out of reach.
